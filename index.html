Now You See It by Dangerous Objects explores the relationship between design, the community itself, and the role design has in the world.

Overall, I think the article brought up the key fact that design cannot exist in a bubble.

It is clear that design has been and will always be influenced by the culture it is surrounded by.

In large the part the Western European lens has completed dominated the field.
This is pervade in not only the education of graphic design, but also the fake 'neutrality' that designers hold onto, knowingly or not.
